Edètte Restaurant

An elite and relaxing restaurant, run by two young owners who wanted to change something in their lives. Edètte Restaurant is located in Borsa, Maramures, a little town in the beautiful country-Romania. The building and decor are inovative and the atmosphere here is warm and relaxing, with rustic ideas and enjoyable music. You will be amazed by our fresh and tasty food, prepared by a hand of chefs from this places. Edètte Restaurant is famous for traditional food, comfort and the quality special evenings where our clients can enjoy different bands, from hard-rock to jazz and traditional. This restaurant is meant to be a nice opportunity to leave the daily routine and feel special with the tastiest dishes and a friendly staff. Do you need a place to celebrate, to relax, to eat something good or to have a date? Edètte restaurant will always be the best choice! You are always welcomed!
